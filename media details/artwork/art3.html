• Infinity Mirrored Room-The Souls of Millions of Light Years Away

For such a simple symbol, the dot on the artwork has a powerful and adaptable quality. It’s an optical effect to make the light shimmer and move across the canvas and it can in-fill a shape or grid which resolves into an image as the viewer moves away and refocuses their eye. The concept of this artwork is basically the eternal unlimited universe, love for humanity and longing for peace in the world which became increasingly serious through the development of Kusama’s philosophy of life and art.
